connectToFirstLedger

  • connectToFirstLedger(): Promise<TransportNodeHid>
    • Defined in exchange.ts:49

    Tries to find a Ledger Nano S and connects to it.

    Each step is retried up to 14 times and the max sleep time per step is (10+20+30+...+120+130)ms. I.e. the worst case runtime of this function is about 2 seconds.

    Returns Promise<TransportNodeHid>
